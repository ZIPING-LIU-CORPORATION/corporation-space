Federal law requires that most group health plans (including the EAP and nurse advice line coverage offered under the Plan) give employees the opportunity to continue their group health plan coverage if there is a “qualifying event” that would result in a loss of coverage. The Continuation Coverage automatically provided to you meets requirements under federal law and is provided in lieu of coverage that would otherwise be required.
